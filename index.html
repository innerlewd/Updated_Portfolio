A Night In

A Night In is a project that me and three other people worked on (Ali Rahimlou, Seth Dousman, and Nicholas Upshaw). This application allows people to enter what ingredients they have in their fridge and choose what type of movie genres they are into and it'd display a randomly-generated movie and three different recipes they could use with the ingredients they have provided.
